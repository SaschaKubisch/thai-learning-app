# v0.6.0 -- Complete Thai Alphabet Coverage

**Release date:** 2026-03-03
**Tag:** v0.6.0

## Summary

Achieves full coverage of all 44 Thai consonants and adds 10 missing common vowels across lessons R01-R15. Rewrites R12-R15 with new lesson themes (compound vowels, alphabet completion, reading consolidation, phrase building). Fixes assessment question quality across all 30 lessons by replacing self-evident, trivia, and duplicate questions with reading comprehension and vocabulary recall questions.

## Changes

### Content -- New Consonants (18 added)

- R07: Added ซ (low-class "s", same sound as ส)
- R09: Added ธ (th, low) and ภ (ph, low) with vocab ธนาคาร, ภาษา
- R10: Added ญ (y, low) with vocab หญิง; updated consonant class table
- R11: Added ศ and ษ (high-class "s" letters); new "Sound Twins Across Classes" theme
- R12: Added ฐ (th, high) and ฮ (h, low) in new compound vowels lesson
- R13: New "Completing the Alphabet" lesson teaching ฎ ฏ ฆ ฌ ฑ ฒ ณ ฬ with real vocabulary (กฎหมาย, ปฏิบัติ, เณร, จุฬา, ผู้เฒ่า, ฆาตกร); acknowledges obsolete ฃ ฅ

### Content -- New Vowels (10 added)

- R04: Added short vowels -ะ, เ-ะ, แ-ะ (teaches long/short pairs)
- R05: Added -ึ (ue short) and -ือ (ue long) with vocab มือ, คือ, นึก, ชื่อ
- R09: Added ใ- vowel form with ไ-/ใ- distinction
- R12: Added compound vowels เ-ีย (ia), -ัว (ua), เ-ือ (uea), เ-อ (oe) with vocab เรียน, ตัว, เมือง, เธอ

### Content -- Lesson Restructure (R12-R15)

- R12: Rewritten as "Compound Vowels" (was "Reading Real Words")
- R13: Rewritten as "Completing the Alphabet" (was "Building Phrases")
- R14: Rewritten as "Reading Real Words" -- consolidation lesson
- R15: Rewritten as "Building Phrases" -- phrase construction with sign reading

### Content -- Assessment Quality Fixes (all 30 lessons)

- R01-R03: Replaced self-evident vowel position questions with vocabulary/reading questions
- R04-R05: New assessments covering expanded vowel content
- R07, R09-R15: New assessments covering added consonants and vowels
- R16: Replaced "How many tones?" trivia with reading comprehension
- R19: Replaced "How many tone marks?" trivia with tone application question
- R21: Replaced duplicate question with SVO construction question
- R27: Replaced cultural trivia with restaurant phrase reading
- R29: Replaced cultural trivia with shopping phrase reading
